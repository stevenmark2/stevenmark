About

I'm an Electrical & Computer Engineering student at UT Austin (graduating May 2029) focused on embedded systems, PCB design, and power electronics. I build real hardware — boards that solve real problems, fly on spacecraft, and drive motors on robots.

Before UT, I researched at MIT's Plasma Science and Fusion Center — fabricating Spin-Orbit Torque magnetic switching devices via e-beam evaporation, optical lithography, and characterizing them on a cryogenic PPMS system.

At UT, I design flight hardware for the CubeSat team and am an incoming hardware intern at Kurtin Robotics. My personal projects span power conversion, motor control, RF, and sensors — all documented with schematics, layout files, and build notes.
