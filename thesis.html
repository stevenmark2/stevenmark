THE LIABILITY AND LEGAL PERSONHOOD OF FULLY AUTONOMOUS VEHICLES

Fully autonomous vehicles should be assigned legal liability and recognized as independent entities. Given the vehicles' real-time decision-making independent from human control, the current legal frameworks are not sufficient. Therefore, new liability frameworks and insurance incentives need to be developed to promote safer technology while also ensuring victims are compensated.
